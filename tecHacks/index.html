Why Do We Need Indian Programming Renaissance??

It is suprising to know that only one in ten start coding before the age of 15

Computer education is still not accessible in India and algorithm and problem solving rarely come up in middle/high school

School level students are not very well motivated to do coding in India.Programming is considered just an optional subject, primary focus of students is Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology that helps them to secure seat in college.

Students are not aware of the underlying abyss of opportunities in this field, very few students know about the competitions and scholarships.
